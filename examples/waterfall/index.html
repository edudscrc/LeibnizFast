LeibnizFast — DAS Waterfall

Simulated DAS (Distributed Acoustic Sensing) acquisition streamed from a C++ generator over ZeroMQ to a Python WebSocket bridge. The generator models a fiber segment with physics-based parameters (sampling rate, repetition rate, spatial downsampling). New data appears on the right; old data scrolls left. Pan and zoom to explore; hover for per-cell values.
